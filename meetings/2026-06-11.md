* USB will be provided the day of the training with an already configured Kali Linux OVA image and lab materials.
* Laptop with at least 8 GB RAM and 80 GB free disk space
* VMware Workstation or similar utility capable of running provided OVA image
* Note that due to architecture, Macs will not be supported
