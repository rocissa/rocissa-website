Discover how leveraging compliance frameworks can strengthen your cybersecurity posture. This session shows how following these frameworks not only meets regulatory demands but also empowers security teams to work smarter, respond faster, and stay resilient, even when outnumbered.

Paul Whittier is an accomplished cybersecurity leader with over 25 years of experience in the technology sector. As the Principal Cybersecurity Advisor at N-Able, Paul collaborates with account managers across various sectors, including banking, ensuring top-tier cybersecurity solutions for clients. Over the years, he has held leadership positions at Adlumin, Checkpoint, Sophos, and Dell SonicWALL, as well as Novell, consistently delivering exceptional customer satisfaction. Paul has been a speaker at numerous conferences and possesses an excellent understanding of the security landscape, as well as the necessary steps to stay protected in an ever-evolving environment
