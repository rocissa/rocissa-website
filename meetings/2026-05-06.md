In a landscape crowded with theoretical "AI Visionaries," the Rochester ISSA is cutting through the noise to deliver strategic clarity. We invite you to a half-day intensive designed to move your organization from AI curiosity or confusion to operational readiness.

This event focuses on the three pillars that define modern cybersecurity leadership:
Operationalizing AI, Hardening against AI-driven threats, and Governing the "Black Box."
