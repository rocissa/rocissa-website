## About The Event

Enjoy and engaging, community-centered evening led by Chris Cooley (Cooley Comics) and Paul Robinson (Tempus Network).  Together we'll explore the unsung heroism behind the cybersecurity profession, the mental and physical challenges, the victories, and the shared sense of purpose that unites us.

## What to expect

- A fun interactive discussion with peers
- Reflections on real-world hero stories
- Opportunities to connect, relax, and recharge
- A custom Super Hero card made just for you, revealed at the event!
